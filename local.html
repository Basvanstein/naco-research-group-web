Venue

FOGA 2025 will be held at the new Gorleaus building, Einsteinweg 55, 2333 CC Leiden, The Netherlands. The university is located near the centre of Leiden, providing easy access to various transport options.

First Arrival

When arriving by train: From Leiden Central Station, you can take a bus or a short walk to the new Gorleaus building. Detailed instructions on reaching the campus will be provided closer to the conference date.

Rooms

All of FOGA will take place on the first floor. Follow the FOGA signs in the Gorleaus building.

Hotel Recommendations

  • Hilton Garden Inn (5 minutes walking)
  • Golden Tulip Leiden Centre (15 minutes by public transport)
  • IBIS Leiden Centre (10 minutes by public transport)
  • Van Der Valk Hotel Leiden (20 minutes walking)

Other Practical Information

  • Tap water is drinkable
  • Tipping is expected in restaurants
  • There is no ATM close to the conference venue
  • Eduroam is available, and we also have guest Wi-Fi
